About TrkElnIt

TrkElnIt is an applied AI and automation studio founded by Rafik Khelifa Toudjine and based in Toronto. The focus is on building controlled, production-grade systems that handle data, decisions, and delivery.

Typical engagements include operational intelligence for real estate, reporting pipelines for finance-driven teams, internal assistants for service organizations, and document handling / interpretation for healthcare-style workflows. In all cases, the goal is the same: connect information, remove bottlenecks, and give teams direct visibility into what matters.

The studio works end-to-end: browser-level automation, backend logic, AI reasoning, reporting surface, and deployment. The output is not a prototype — it’s infrastructure the client can actually run.

Privacy and locality are built in. When needed, language models run on Ollama using Llama / Mistral instead of external APIs. Dashboards are delivered through Streamlit and Google Sheets for clarity and auditability. Workloads are hosted on Oracle Cloud or Google Cloud with monitoring and version control.

TrkElnIt is intentionally lean. The model is simple: understand the workflow, build the system, hand over something real.
