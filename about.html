About

Controlled business systems, not demo automation.

TrkElnIt builds applied AI, backend workflows, document intelligence, CRM structure, and delivery-ready operational systems for teams that need something real to run.

TrkElnIt is an applied AI and automation studio founded by Rafik Khelifa Toudjine in Toronto. The focus is on building production systems that connect documents, workflows, reporting, payment state, and operational decisions into one controlled environment.

Typical delivery includes private AI workflows, OCR and parsing pipelines, backend APIs, CRM tooling, payment tracking, dashboards, and deployment infrastructure. The goal is not experimentation. The goal is operational leverage with clear ownership and traceable state.

The studio works end to end: client intake, payment routing, data extraction, internal interfaces, backend orchestration, and deployment. Each engagement is meant to produce infrastructure a team can actually use after handoff.
