Capabilities

Browser Automation

Playwright-driven agents that can authenticate, navigate business platforms, read relevant data, and feed internal systems. Built for reliability and auditability, not scraping at scale.

Document Parsing & AI Extraction

LangChain pipelines that read PDFs, forms, statements, onboarding packets and produce structured fields. Includes retrieval-augmented answering over client documents using local Llama / Mistral through Ollama.

Internal Assistants / Agents

Private AI agents that respond to users, follow conversation state, update records, and hand off when the task is done. Not a generic chatbot — task-scoped assistants that run inside the client's environment.

Dashboards & Reporting

Streamlit dashboards for operations, finance, inventory, or pipeline visibility. Daily reporting via Google Sheets, PDF, and email delivery through Gmail API.

Backend & Workflow Layer

FastAPI services for business logic and data APIs. n8n automation to coordinate triggers, escalation, messaging, and scheduling between systems.

Deployment

Oracle Cloud and Google Cloud environments for sustained runtime. Containerized services, monitored jobs, controlled cost.
